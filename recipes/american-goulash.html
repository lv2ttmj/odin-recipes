American Goulash

The American Goulash is a delicious noodle stew with a mash of flavors including ground beef, tomatoes, and loads of seasoning.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 2 pounds of ground beef
  • 1 large diced onion
  • cloves of minced garlic
  • 2 large bay leaves
  • 2 tablespoons of paprika
  • 2 tesapoons of italian seasoning
  • 2 teaspoons of kosher salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground black pepper
  • 1 pinch of cayenne pepper, or to taste
  • 1 quart of chicken broth
  • 1 24-ounce jar of marinara sauce
  • 1 15-ounce can of diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup of water
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 2 cups of elbow macaroni
  • 1/4 cup of chopped italian parsley
  • 1 cup of shredded white cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in a pot over medium-high heat. Add your ground beef and onion, cooking and stirring until the beef is browned and the onion is translucent, which will take around 5 minutes. Continue to cook and stir until the liquid is evaporated.
  2. Add your garlic, bay leaves, paprika, italian seasoning, salt, black pepper, and cayenne and cook, stirring occasionally for about 3 minutes.
  3. Pour in your broth, marinara sauce, and diced tomatoes. Pour water into the sauce jar, swirl those together and then pour it into the pot. Stir in soy sauce, and bring it to a simmer. Reduce the heat to medium and simmer for about 30 minutes.
  4. Increase the heat to medium-high and stir in the macaroni. Stir occasionally until just barely tender, which will take around 12 minutes. Check again after 10 minutes.
  5. Turn off the heat and remove the bay leaves. Stir in cheese and parsley. Cover and let rest for 5 minutes.
