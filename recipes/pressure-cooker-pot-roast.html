Pressure Cooker Pot Roast

This is an easy-to-make savory and delicious meal giving us a tender beef.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • A 3-pound trimmed boneless beef chuck roast
  • Black pepper, salt, and onion powder
  • 4.5 ounces of store-bought or homemade beef broth
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons of Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 Onion
  • 4 Carrots
  • 4 Potatoes

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in the pressure cooker and sear the meat on all sides.
  2. Season the roast with a pinch of each of the seasonings, then add the broth, sauce, and onion. Seal the lid.
  3. Reduce the heat to low and cook on full pressure for 30 minutes.
  4. Lower the pressure, add the carrots and potatoes after cutting them, and seal the lid.
  5. Return to full pressure and cook for 15 more minutes.
